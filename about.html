About the CMS Healthcare Dashboard

Project Purpose

The Healthcare Metrics Dashboard is a user-friendly web application that helps transform complex CMS datasets into simple, interactive charts. Its mission is to make national healthcare performance data more transparent, accessible, and actionable.

The CMS Healthcare Dashboard turns publicly available data from the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS) into accessible, visual insights. This tool helps people better understand key healthcare performance metrics such as emergency department wait times, vaccination coverage, and sepsis care.

A long ED wait can mean delayed care; low vaccination rates may signal risks for both staff and patients; and sepsis treatment compliance impacts critical survival outcomes. This dashboard educates users on why each of these performance measures matters.

The data supports decision-making across roles:

  • Hospital teams can identify areas for quality improvement.
  • Patients can gain more transparency into their care systems.
  • Public health agencies and researchers can explore broader trends and equity issues.
